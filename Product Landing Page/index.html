Features

Two built-in microphones.

Make sure your friends hear you online through the two built-in microphones, which are optimally positioned to capture your voice perfectly, with advanced noise-canceling technology.

Easy to use controls.

Adjust your setup as you play with the built-in mute, volume and audio buttons. Easily check how your friends are listening to you with the dedicated microphone monitoring button.

3.5mm connector.

Simply connect the headset to your mobile devices with the 3.5mm jack audio cable.

Bluetooth

Enjoy up to 12 hours of cordless use thanks to the built-in rechargeable battery. Use the included adapter to connect to PS5 and PS4™ consoles, as well as compatible Windows and macOS® computers.
